Requirements gap note for the other three roles
Source checked: P04 - E-Commerce Store 2 (2).docx
Customer role status: customer-side missing items were addressed separately in this branch.

Role 2 - Seller / Vendor
Already visible in the codebase: vendor profile/settings, inventory/product add-edit-delete, multiple product images, stock quantity, low-stock display, coupon create/toggle, order item confirm/ship actions, review replies, analytics, and earnings screens.

Still needs to be checked or finished:
- Seller registration must collect shop name, description, address, and logo, then stay pending until admin approval.
- Vendor login must be blocked until the seller is approved.
- Product delete must be blocked when the product is linked to pending orders.
- Order shipping needs a tracking note saved somewhere visible to customer/delivery manager; the shared schema does not currently define a tracking_note column.
- Return request management for seller products needs approve/reject with a reason.
- Vendor order detail should show customer shipping address, all items from that store, and payment method in one detail view.
- Analytics should be verified against real order data for total revenue per period, top-selling products, order volume by day/week/month, and average order value.
- Earnings should be verified for total earned, platform commission deducted, and net payout per period.
- Every vendor form/action should be checked for server-side validation and prepared statements.
- Confirm at least one vendor feature is AJAX-based and returns JSON.

Role 3 - Delivery Manager
Current repo scan did not show a delivery-manager controller/view/dashboard implementation.

Needs to be implemented:
- Delivery manager login/profile and role-based dashboard.
- Logistics dashboard showing pending dispatch count, active deliveries, and delivered today.
- Delivery agent management: add, edit, activate/deactivate agents.
- Agent list with current active-deliveries count.
- Delivery zone management: add, edit, delete, fee, and estimated days.
- Ready-for-dispatch orders list once seller items are shipped and not assigned.
- Assign delivery agent to order by zone/availability.
- Active deliveries page with order ID, zone, assigned agent, current status, and time since assignment.
- Delivery status updates: Picked Up, In Transit, Delivered, Failed.
- Failed delivery handling with reason, reassignment, or customer notification/status update.
- Delivery history with completed and failed deliveries.
- Agent performance report: completed count, average delivery time, failed delivery rate.
- Zone performance report: delivery volume and average time per zone.
- Daily and weekly delivery summary.
- At least one AJAX JSON endpoint.
- Server-side validation and prepared statements for every write action.

Role 4 - Platform Admin
Already visible in the codebase: admin dashboard shell, vendor approval/rejection, category management, and dispute handling.

Still needs to be checked or finished:
- Dashboard numbers must include total users by role, active sellers, orders today, and platform revenue this month.
- Seller rejection should store/display a rejection reason if the report requires it.
- Seller suspension/reactivation should be distinct from initial approval/rejection if possible.
- Customer and delivery manager account management: search, view, deactivate, reactivate.
- Product moderation: view all products, search/filter by category or seller, remove policy-violating listings.
- Order management: view all orders with filters by status, date range, seller, and customer.
- Platform commission override per seller.
- Platform-level coupon campaigns applied at checkout.
- Platform-wide revenue analytics: GMV, commission earned, top sellers, top categories.
- Platform-wide delivery performance overview from delivery data.
- Featured products and seller banners for the marketplace homepage.
- Platform-wide announcements to all users.
- Monthly comprehensive reports.
- Printable/exportable HTML report output.
- Confirm every admin write action uses prepared statements, server-side validation, and role-based access control.
- Confirm at least one admin feature is AJAX-based and returns JSON.
